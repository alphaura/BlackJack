How to play BlackJack;

The goal of Blackjack is to collect cards that total more points than the dealer, without going over 21. The first one that goes over 21 busts and is out of the game. Dealer stays at 17, player decides when to stay with the hand they like.

Game Functionality;

  • The trigger section (middle white box), is there to guide you on the state of the game
  • Press on "Start Game!" to start a new game
  • If you wish to take a new card, press "Hit Me!" and this will generate a random card for you
  • If you wish to stay with the current hand press "Stay", which this will then give the dealer a card. Keep clicking on it until the dealer's total reaches 17 or higher.
